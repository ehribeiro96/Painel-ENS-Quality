Executive summary

The product frontend is split between a compatibility shell and an isolated target experience.

The compatibility shell is still active and owns broad operational routes, but Apoema is the clearer long-term target because it already has a scoped shell, a separate route subtree, and a dedicated chat adapter with explicit fallback rules.

Most other frontend surfaces are either support layers, archives, or frozen snapshots. They should remain documented but should not be treated as the target shape of the product.
